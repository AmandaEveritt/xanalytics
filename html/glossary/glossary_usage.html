Glossary: Usage Statistics

Usage Statistics are calculated using a combination of the edX studentmodule table and tracking log data. Registered, Viewed, Explored are determined from the edX studentmodule table. Certification and Verified ID data are determined using edX certficiates table. Male and Female statistics are determined using edX profile table.
  • Registered
    • Number of participants who have at least registered in the course. This # includes participants who have registered, viewed, explored and certified.
  • Viewed
    • Number of participants who have at least clicked once into the courseware. This # includes participants who registered and viewed the course.
  • Explored
    • Number of participants who viewed at least half of the course chapters. For instance, if there are 10 chapters, and the user clicked once into 5 of the chapters, this user is marked as exploring the course. This does not necessarily indicate that the user explored half of the course materials. This # includes participants who registered, viewed and explored the course.
  • Un-Registered
    • Number of participants who un-enrolled from the course.
  • Net Registered
    • Number of participants who are left after removing un-rollees.
  • Certified
    • Number of participants who are certified in total so far. Note that prior to Dec 2015, this # included users who passed the course if the enrollment mode was honor/audit. Currently, only users who enrolled as ID verified can receive a certificate. Users who passed the course but did not enroll as ID verified are excluded. This # includes participants who registered, viewed, explored and certified for this course.
  • Verified IDs
    • Number of participants who has verified IDs.
  • Certified and Verified
    • Number of participants who received certifications and verified IDs.
  • Male
    • Number of participants who reported as male in the edX profile.
  • Female
    • Number of participants who reported as female in the edX profile.
