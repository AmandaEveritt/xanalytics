Glossary: Enrollment by day for Course

  • Ever registered
    • This includes the cumulative number of registrants in total. It also includes people who un-enroll.
  • Net registered
    • This includes the cumulative number of registrants without the number of registrants who un-enroll. Therefore, this number is smaller than "ever registered."
  • Ever reg registered daily
    • This includes the number of registrants without the number of registrants who un-enroll by day.
