Glossary: Time on Task

All time-on-task calculations are computed using a 30 minute time-out threshold, established by the Interactive Advertising Bureau (2009) and Google (2013). Participant time is counted from the first logged event, or "session start", until the "session end", defined as the next logged event following which 30 minutes passes with no logged activity. Three events interspersed by 10 minutes will count as a 20-minute session. A single event isoloated temporally by more than 30 minutes is not counted.
