Glossary: Top Worldwide Countries Pie Charts

Geo-location information is obtained using a user's modal ip address. Note that edX profile location is not used. Additionally, not all ip addresses can be mapped to a location, but most are captured using multiple geo-location databases. Using IP addresses instead of open text fields is more consistent and readily available for a majority of the users that interact with the course. Users who just registered and did not interact with the course (viewers) are not counted in this data.
  • Registrants by Country
    • Pie chart showing % of Registrants from the top countries. This % of registrants includes users who at leasted registered for a course (thus including users who viewed, explored and certified).
  • Viewers by Country
    • Pie chart showing % of Viewers from the top countries. This % of viewers includes users who at least viewed a course (thus including users who may have also explored and certified).
  • Certified by Country
    • Pie chart showing % of Certified from the top countries. This % of certified includes users who certified in a course.
  • ID Verified by Country
    • Pie chart showing % of ID Verified from the top countries. This % of ID verified includes users does not indicate level of completion.
