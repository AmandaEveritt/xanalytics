Glossary: Participant Enrollment by Day for course

  • Ever participated
    • This includes the cumulative number of registrants who have clicked into the courseware at least once. It also includes people who un-enroll.
  • Net participated
    • This includes the cumulative number of registrants who have clicked into the courseware at least once without the number of registraints who un-enroll. Therefore, this number is smaller than "ever participated."
  • Ever participated daily
    • This includes the number of participations daily in the course.
