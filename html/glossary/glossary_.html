Glossary: TBD

  • chapter
    • Number of chapters in the course. Chapters are top-level sections defined in the course outline. These can be viewed in the left navigation bar of the course page.
  • sequential
    • Number of sequentials in the course. Sequentials are embedded under chapters, and can be accessed through the left navigation bar in the course page.
  • vertical
    • Number of verticals in the course. Verticals are embedded under sequentials, and can be accessed through clicking on a vertical element.
  • html
    • Number of html pages in the course. Html pages are the page sections in vertical that do not include videos or problems
  • video
    • Number of videos in the course.
  • problem
    • Number of promblem assets in the course. Problems are a type of component.
  • survey
    • Number of surveys embedded in the course. Surveys are a type of components.
